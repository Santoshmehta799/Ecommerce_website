How it works

Learn from the beginning how we help you make money.


Setup your Profile

  • Start by registering yourself as a seller at seller.seventhsq.com This creates an account for yourself from where you can upload your products, manage orders and receive payments.
  • After completing registration, it's best to complete your profile, so you don't have to worry about the same details repeatedly.
  • Once profile is completed, start {% if user.is_authenticated %} Adding New Products. {% else %} Adding New Product {% endif %} Better the info, better they would be perceived by the customer. If you're new and want some help, please reach out to us at seller@seventhsq.com and we'll guide you through it.

Check out our Registration and Account FAQs for related questions.


Manage orders

  • Your added products will shortly be live on the platform and ready for purchases.
  • When a customer purchases your product, we'll notify you with details over your seller account, phone and email. Be sure to check these regularly.
  • Once received the order, you can start packaging and coordinate delivery.
  • At every stage, we update the customer and estimated delivery time.
  • Timely dispatch is important to build customer's trust.

Check out our Processing Orders FAQs for related questions.


Receive Payments

  • On purchase, a customer can pay through multiple options including cards, net banking, e-wallets and Cash-on-delivery.
  • We collect the payments through all the methods and transfer them to your bank account.
  • This takes place on successful delivery to the customer, as per payments policy.

Check out our Pricing & Payments and Shipping & Returns FAQs for related questions.
