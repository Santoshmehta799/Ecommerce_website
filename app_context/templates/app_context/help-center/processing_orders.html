Processing Orders

Once you've successfully listed your products on the platform, the visitors will be able to see your products and purchase them. Find out everything related to orders on this page.



Order Journey

  1. Customer places order for your product on the marketplace portal.
  2. Order received for your product is displayed on the portal under 'Current Orders'.
  3. You are also duly notified on the portal, phone, whatsapp, email as per your settings.
  4. Check order details and prepare to dispatch by / before the ship by date. This includes Shipping Label (as applicable), invoice, packaging (as applicable) and Delivery Challan / E-Way Bills (as applicable).
  5. Keep the customer updated marking the status in Current Orders including once order is dispatched.
  6. Check the Payment History page once order is successfully delivered.

Enquiries

Building Materials Sales is often personalised based on Quantity and Delivery Location. Many products also have fluctuating pricing which makes them unable to list at fixed prices.
We have solved this issue for both the consumers and the seller by offering to the consumers industry-level procurement tools such as Request for Quote.
Once an enquiry is raised for your product, it is displayed under 'Customer Enquiry ' with all the details to help you decide whether you want to fulfil the enquiry or not.

Order Terms & Settings

-order Processing Time

Order Processing Time or Order Handling time refers to the time it takes for you or any of your products to be packed for shipment. You can set this time for all your products as default in your seller profile or for individual products while you upload them.

-order cutoff Time

This represents the time when you'll last accept orders for the day. It is useful in estimating shipping and delivery timelines.

For example,
If your warehouse labour for shipping new orders leaves at 5pm everyday. Then you can set your order cut off time for 5pm. In this way, even if you're dispatching the product on the same day for the rest of the orders, every order received after 5pm will automatically be required to be delivered the next day only.

Read more about Order Processing Time and Order Cut off Time.

Sampling

Some Building Materials often require sampling as part of the purchase process. In this scenario, sellers for the segments have worked over expectations in providing samples to their prospecting customers.
We 've made it easier for you. For products requiring sampling from any buyer on Seventh Square, we'll arrange the pick and drop for you, so you can increase your sales even in such a product segment.
To enable sampling for your account, please reach out to us at seller@seventhsq.com or contact your relationship manager.

Guidelines

  1. Respond to every order received as soon as possible. Every Customer awaits updates on their order and providing these are form a big part of Customer Experience online. Whether you want to cancel the order , delay shipment or prepone delivery, update your response.
  2. Keep your contact information up to date so you never miss out on any order or payment updates sent by us. Also, Check your phone / email regularly in case you miss an update
