Manage your Account

Once you've registered your account, you will need to complete your profile and adjust your settings and info. In this page, we'll guide you to create your profile to extract the most benefits out of it.


Completing your Profile

Some info for your profile is collected during registration, such as phone number, email, representative & company name and GSTIN. For rest of the details, you have to fill them in after registration.
Here's a list of information that is required to be filled :

  • Bank Details
  • GST & Company verification Document upload
  • Registered Address
  • Pick up location(s)

While at your profile page, you'll also see some Account setting options :

  • Default Order Processing Time
  • Order Cut-off time
  • Notifications & Communication settings

You can set these to your convenience and processes at the start once and these will reflect on all orders received or products uploaded.


While most of the info can be easily edited once registered, some information requires verification or approval for successfully editing.

Account Verification

At Seventh Square, we endeavour to create a trustworthy and safe trade ecosystem. To ensure this, we carry out verification of the info provided by our sellers so that only genuine sellers can onboard. However, this process is very simple and seller doesn't have to do anything extra. All checks are carried out automatically in background, maintaining utmost convenience and simplicity.
