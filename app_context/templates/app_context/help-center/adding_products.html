Adding Products

After completing your profile, you can add and list your products online. Adding your portfolio on seventh Square is extremely easy to understand. Follow this page to learn how to upload your portfolio.



How to Add Product

Follow this step to list your product to Seveth Square:

  • login to your account
  • Go to 'Add new Product ' in the left menu bar
  • Fill in the required details, review and submit
  • Check Added products under ' Active Products'
  • Review Products, Finalise Edits and list it online on the Active Product page

Adding Product Form

We've described the Add new Product form here to help you navigate the form better. Please find the info required and field descriptions below. To understand any field in detail, please click on the field name below :

Section - 1 : Basic Info

  • Product Title
  • Product Category & Type
  • Brand Name
  • HSN Code
  • Shipping Method
  • Images

Section - 2 : More Info

  • About the Brand
  • Storage
  • Usage
  • Installation
  • Handling
  • Warranty & Guarantee
  • Minimum Order Quantity
  • Variants

Section - 3 : Pricing

  • Price on Request
  • MRP & Sale Price
  • Taxes & Shipping
  • Packaging
  • Components per unit

Section - 4 : Shipping

  • Product Weight
  • Country of Origin
  • Returnable Product
  • Product & Packed Box Dimensions
  • Material
  • Serviced Regions
  • Pick Up Location

Guidelines while Adding Products:

Follow these guidelines while adding new products :

  • Provide Accurate Information for the Products
  • Provide complete Information for the Products
  • Never Misrepresent any info related to the products
  • Follow Trademark and Copyright rules
  • Keep Inventory Updated and update on the portal when out of Inventory for any product
