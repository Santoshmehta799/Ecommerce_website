Shipping & Returns

Shipping forms an important part of your journey in E-Commerce as well as Customer Experience. Learn all about Delivering your products to your customers with Seventh Square



Shipping Options

For shipping orders on Seventh Square, you can either ship them yourself under 'Self Shipping' or 'Ship with Seventh Square'

Self Shipping

In this selection, you have your own courier or tied-up with a logistics provider to deliver your online orders. If you select Self Shipping, your shipping costs have to be included in the sale price, so when you're adding your products on the portal, make sure to adjust for your average shipping costs (If you've added already, you can edit it again here) .

Shipping with seventh square

Seventh Square has tied up with many leading logistics solutions providers in India to help you deliver your products to your customers on the portal. With this, we are providing you with quality service and competitive & dynamic pricing to fulfill your delivery requirements. Along with regular ecommerce shipments, we also provide delivery for Bulky building materials as per your requirements. In this selection, the shipping costs can be both inclusive and exclusive of the sale price, as per your choice while you add your product.

Pricing:

Seventh Square Shipping Prices are based on product weight and distance to be covered. These prices are accessible in your seller agreement.

Selecting your preferred and shipping option

You can select your preferred Shipping option for each product when you add the product to the portal in the Add new product form.

Returns

Returns are a normal part of selling whether online or offline However, not all products can be returned by nature, including bulky materials, fragile items or customised products. Since, this industry is vast and all products have different features, we have provided you with the option to select whether your product is returnable or not.
All returnable products come under a general return policy of the platform which ensures fair trade and satisfactory transaction between both parties. However, you can also set your own return policy which will be duly displayed to the visitors.
